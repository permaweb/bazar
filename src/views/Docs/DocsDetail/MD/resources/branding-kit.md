### Brand Guidelines

#### Logo Usage

**Do:**

- Use official logo files from the brand kit
- Maintain proper clear space around the logo
- Use approved color variations
- Scale proportionally

**Don't:**

- Alter logo colors outside approved palette
- Distort or skew the logo
- Add effects or shadows
- Place on busy backgrounds without proper contrast

#### Color Palette

Our colors represent the permanence and innovation of the Arweave ecosystem:

- Use primary colors for main brand elements
- Secondary colors for accents and highlights
- Ensure proper contrast for accessibility

#### Typography

Consistent typography creates a cohesive brand experience:

- Use specified font families from the brand kit
- Follow hierarchy guidelines
- Maintain readability across all platforms

### Using BazAR Branding

#### For Community Members

- Feel free to use BazAR branding when promoting the platform
