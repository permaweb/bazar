## Welcome to Bazar

Bazar is the permaweb orderbook for trading AO-based tokens built on Arweave.

Trades are executed on [HyperBEAM](https://hyperbeam.ar.io/) with no intermediaries. Bazar is currently in beta on HyperBEAM. Some adjustments are still in progress.

For questions or feedback, open a ticket in this [Discord](https://discord.com/invite/rMQG8utY).
